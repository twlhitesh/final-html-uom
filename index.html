About Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is a state in the northern part of India. It is known for its scenic beauty, picturesque landscapes, and rich cultural heritage.

Tourism in Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh offers a variety of tourist attractions including hill stations like Shimla, Manali, and Dharamshala, adventure sports, religious sites, and wildlife sanctuaries.
